=================================================================
RINGKASAN EKSEKUTIF
=================================================================

Timber CRM adalah platform SaaS manajemen hubungan pelanggan (CRM) yang
dirancang khusus untuk industri retail mebel kayu dan showroom furnitur
di Indonesia. Platform ini mengintegrasikan pipeline penjualan kustom,
sistem loyalty membership, manajemen stok bahan material kayu, pelacakan
pengiriman armada, dan otomatisasi kampanye marketing.

    Multiplier: Natural x1.0 | Melamine x1.15 | PU Lacquer x1.25
    Total = Base x Multiplier x Quantity
    Status: <5 = KRITIS | 5-25 = OPTIMAL | >25 = OVERCAPACITY

--- V3.4 INTERACTIVE ROI CALCULATOR ---

Input Slider:
  Jumlah Sales Team : 1-30 orang (default 5)
  Omzet Bulanan     : Rp 20Jt - Rp 1Milyar (default Rp 150Jt)

Formula Kalkulasi:
  timeSaved         = salesTeam x 18 jam/bulan
  potentialIncrease = revenue x 0.26 (proyeksi +26% closing rate)
  adminCostSaved    = timeSaved x Rp 65.000/jam
  totalImpact       = potentialIncrease + adminCostSaved

Output Ditampilkan:
  - Jam kerja dihemat per bulan
  - Proyeksi kenaikan omzet (+Rp XJt)
  - Total dampak finansial estimasi
